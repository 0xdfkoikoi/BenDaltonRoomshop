about us

Secondhand to Signature — This Is Our Threaded Story

In 2018, we began not with a storefront, but with a passion — discovering forgotten garments, hidden in thrift piles and night markets. Every jacket, shirt, and trouser had a story. We just gave them a second chance.

Start in 2020, the mission evolved. we moved beyond curated old pieces - we started designed our own. with every fabric cut and every stitch sewn, bendalton roomshop transformed into a garment studio rooted in character, texture and minimal form. we create clothes that are easy to wear, hard to forget
