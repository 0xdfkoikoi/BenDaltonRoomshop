Bendalton Room Shop started its journey in 2018 as a passionate reseller of secondhand thrift clothing. What began as a small venture quickly grew into a movement — blending vintage finds with timeless streetwear vibes. By 2020, we shifted fully into the garment business, producing to our client needs. With a focus on quality, minimalism, and character, our creations have reached fashion lovers across the globe. Today, we proudly serve 15+ loyal customers from various nation

How We Work

1. Choose Your Design
‍Browse our past drops or request a custom cut — from outerwear to shirts, we’re ready to build your look.
2. Pick Fabric & Color
‍We’ll show you available materials, textures, and color options — picked to match your vibe and comfort.
3. Place Your Order
‍Once your design is locked, we’ll confirm sizing, production time, and pricing. Each piece is made in small batches or one-on-one — never rushed.
4. Reach Out Anytime
We’ll talk through your ideas, explore design possibilities, and prepare a mockup tailored to your project. From fabric suggestions to final fit — we’ll make sure everything feels just right before we begin. Contact us now
